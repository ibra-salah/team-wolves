FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a marketing background to join?
Not at all. This course is designed for complete beginners as well as those with some marketing experience who want to go deeper into digital channels. We start from the foundations and progressively build toward advanced campaign management.
Will I run real ads during the course?
Yes. We guide you through setting up real campaigns on Meta Ads Manager and Google Ads. We provide structured exercises with recommended starting budgets as low as $5–10 per day so you can see real performance data and learn by doing.
Which platforms does the course cover?
The course covers Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn (organic), Google Search Ads, Google Analytics 4, Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, and Canva for visual content creation. We also cover SEO tools like Ahrefs and Google Search Console.
How quickly can I find clients or a job after this course?
Many of our students land their first client before the course is even finished — by starting with friends, family, or local businesses. For employment, most graduates with a solid portfolio of campaign results find marketing roles within 30–60 days of completion.
Is the course in Arabic?
Live sessions are conducted in Arabic for maximum clarity, while all templates, strategy documents, and written materials are available in both Arabic and English. This prepares you to work with both local and international clients.
Is there ongoing support after the course ends?
Yes. You retain lifetime access to the course materials and our private community group. Graduates also get access to monthly Q&A sessions and a dedicated channel for ongoing marketing questions and campaign reviews.
