About This Course

What This Course Covers

The Wolves Team English Communication Course is a structured, results-driven program built specifically for Arabic speakers who want to achieve genuine English fluency. Unlike traditional grammar-heavy approaches, this course is built around real communication — the English you actually need in your career, travels, and daily life.

Every lesson connects a language concept to a real situation — job interviews, business emails, presentations, social conversations, and media comprehension. You will speak English from lesson one, building confidence and accuracy simultaneously through structured drills, conversation practice, and immersive listening exercises.

By graduation, you will hold professional-level conversations in English, write clear business communications, understand native speakers in video, meetings, and media — and possess the vocabulary, pronunciation, and grammar to express yourself precisely and confidently.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this course taught in Arabic or English?
The instruction is in Arabic to ensure you understand every concept clearly. English is the practice language — all exercises, conversations, and output are done in English. This bilingual approach is proven to accelerate fluency for Arabic speakers significantly faster than pure English immersion.
What English level do I need to start?
The course accommodates levels from complete beginner (A1) through intermediate (B2). The first two modules build foundational skills that work for any level, and the later modules branch into more advanced professional and academic use. We recommend a brief placement assessment at enrollment to ensure you start in the right track.
How much English will I be able to speak by the end?
Students who complete all 8 modules and actively practice the speaking assignments typically reach B2 to C1 proficiency — equivalent to upper-intermediate to advanced. This means holding professional conversations, writing business communications, and handling job interviews with confidence.
Are there any live sessions or is it all pre-recorded?
Both. The 72 lessons are pre-recorded and available on-demand for flexible scheduling. In addition, we hold weekly live speaking sessions where students practice conversation with the instructor and classmates. These sessions are recorded for those who can't attend live.
Does this course help with IELTS preparation specifically?
Yes. Module 8 is entirely dedicated to IELTS exam preparation with timed reading, writing tasks with model answers, speaking practice based on band descriptors, and full mock tests with scoring feedback. Students targeting IELTS 6.5+ have consistently achieved their score after completing the full course.
How many hours per week do I need to study?
We recommend 5–7 hours per week — lessons plus practice. English improves through consistent daily exposure, even in short bursts. We provide daily micro-practice exercises (10 minutes) in addition to the main lessons, making it easy to maintain momentum even on busy days.
